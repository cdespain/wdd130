Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Rush of the Rapids is to help share our love of white-water rafting with the world! We will provide timely information and resources to help people of all skill levels take on their next challenge or learn new skills to improve their time on the water!

Audience

Our site can help anyone who has an interest in enjoying rivers, no matter the skill level. Whether you like a mild pace down a beautiful river, or a heart-pumping rush through rapids that will leave you exhilerated, our site here at Rush of the Rapids will prove an invaluable resource for you.
